About Us

Morris Frock Post 42 is an American Legion Post located in Hagerstown MD. We have a full-service kitchen, bar, dining room, and banquet hall.

The lounge is open for lunch Tuesday - Friday and for dinner Tuesday - Saturday.

Call the post to reserve a room for your sepcial event or meeting. If you are currently on active duty, serving the United States honorably, anywhere in the world, or have served honorably during any of the following eligible war eras, we invite you to become a member of The American Legion. National Guard and Reservists having been Federally activated (including basic training) during these same war eras also meet the eligibility requirements. +Aug. 2, 1990 to today (Gulf War / War On Terrorism) +Dec. 20, 1989 to Jan. 31, 1990 (Panama) +Aug. 24, 1982 to July 31, 1984 (Lebanon / Grenada) +Feb. 28, 1961 to May 7, 1975 (Vietnam War) +June 25, 1950 to Jan. 31, 1955 (Korean War) +Dec. 7, 1941 to Dec. 31, 1946 (World War II) +April 6, 1917 to Nov. 11, 1918 (World War I)

Rentals

EVENT AND BANQUET HALL RENTAL AND CATERING POLICIES

Rental includes chairs and tables, basic clean up, set up and take down of tables and chairs, use of foyer. Paper table cover and napkins included. No event to go past 12:00am. All food and beverages must be purchased from American Legion Post 42. An 18% service charge will be added to food and drink purchases. No outside food or beverages allowed with the exception of ceremonial cakes. All facilities are handicap accessible.

Banquet Hall

Capacity: 200 recommended 225 max Fee: $50/hr. min of 4hr. Additional hours must be requested at time of contract Bar: $20/hr. min of 4hr

Dining Room/ SAL room

Capacity: 50 max Fee: $75 for the first 4hr, $20/hr. for additional hours. Additional hours must be requested at time of contract

Pavilion

Capacity: 200+ Fee: $100 for the first 4hr, $20/hr. for additional hours. Additional hours must be requested at time of contract

BOOKING / CANCELLATION POLICY

Security Deposit – 50% room rental is due at the time of contact written to reserve date and will be used towards balance of rental. Event dates are first come, first serve. Cancellation within one month of rental date will result in the forfeiture of the entire deposit. Thirty days prior to event all food and beverage selection need to be discussed with banquet manager and balance of room fee is due. Full payment for the food, beverage, and other charges due day of event but may be made in advance When available, room may be decorated the day before event from 9:00am to 9:00pm Mon – Fri. This time must be scheduled in advance with the banquet manager. If you want to reserve the night before your event you may reserve it for $100 at time of contract.
